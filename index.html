How it works

From the page to the feed, in three steps.

01

Write

Every piece starts as the creator's own original poem — written by hand, then transcribed.

02

Compose

The poem is set in motion as a clean, typewriter-style short video with the signature look.

03

Publish

The finished short is posted to the creator's own accounts — TikTok and YouTube — through the official APIs.

About

“I write small poems and wanted an honest way to share them — so I built a tool that does exactly that, and nothing more.”

A personal studio

Writings of Dhas isn't a marketplace or a network. It's a single creator's tool for publishing their own work — used only by its owner, for their own accounts.

Original work only

Everything published is the creator's own original poetry. The tool does not collect, access, scrape, or post anyone else's content or data.

TikTok integration

Built on TikTok's official Content Posting API.

What the app does: it lets the owner authenticate with their own TikTok account and publish their own poem videos to that account.

What it accesses: only the permission needed to upload and post videos to the connected account (video.upload / video.publish).

What it never does: it does not read, store, or post other users' content, followers, or personal data, and it is not offered to third parties.

Full details are in our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
