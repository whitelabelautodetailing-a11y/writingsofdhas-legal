Privacy Policy

Application: Writings of Dhas
Effective date: June 2, 2026

This application ("the App") is a personal tool used by its sole owner to publish their own original written poetry as short videos to their own social media accounts, including TikTok. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App handles.

1. Who this applies to

The App is operated by a single individual for their own personal content. It is not a multi-user service and does not collect, process, or store data from the public or from any third-party users.

2. Information the App accesses

When the owner connects their TikTok account, TikTok provides the App with:

  • An OAuth access token and refresh token, used solely to upload videos to the owner's own account.
  • Basic account information (such as the account's display name and open ID) returned by the user.info.basic scope.

The App does not access private messages, follower data, other users' content, or any information beyond what is required to upload the owner's own videos.

3. How information is used

Access tokens are used only to authenticate uploads of the owner's own poetry videos to the owner's own TikTok account. The App performs no other action with this data.

4. How information is stored

Tokens are stored locally on the owner's personal computer in a restricted, non-public file. They are never transmitted to any third party other than TikTok's official API endpoints, and they are never sold, shared, or published.

5. Data sharing

The App does not share, sell, or disclose any data to third parties. The only external service it communicates with is TikTok's official API.

6. Data retention and deletion

Stored tokens can be deleted at any time by the owner by removing the local credential file or by revoking the App's access from within TikTok's account settings (Settings → Security → Manage app permissions).

7. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

8. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated; the effective date above will be revised accordingly.
